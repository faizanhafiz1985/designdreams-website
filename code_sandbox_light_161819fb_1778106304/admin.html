Hero Slideshow Images

For each slide, either paste an image URL or switch to Upload from Device to use a photo from your computer. Click Save Changes to apply to the homepage hero slideshow.

Using an Image URL

  • Use Unsplash: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-ID?w=1600&q=90
  • Use Google Drive: Share → "Anyone with link" → direct link
  • Use ImgBB: Upload at imgbb.com → copy "Direct link"
  • Use Cloudinary or any image CDN
  • Recommended: 1600×900px or larger

Uploading from Your Device

  • Switch to the "Upload from Device" tab on any slide
  • Click the zone or drag & drop a JPEG/PNG/WebP file
  • Image is stored locally in your browser — works offline
  • Note: Keep files under ~2 MB per slide for best performance
  • For permanent hosting, consider uploading to ImgBB instead
